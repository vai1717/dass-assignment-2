\section*{\textcolor{blue}{Implemented Modules}}

The following six required modules and two bonus modules were designed and implemented:

\begin{description}
  \item[\textbf{registration.py}] Registers new crew members, storing their name and role. Raises \texttt{ValueError} on duplicate registrations.
  \item[\textbf{crew\_management.py}] Manages roles and records skill levels for each crew member. A member must be registered before a role can be assigned.
  \item[\textbf{inventory.py}] Tracks cars, spare parts, tools, and the team's cash balance.
  \item[\textbf{race\_management.py}] Creates races and enforces that only registered drivers with cars in inventory may participate.
  \item[\textbf{results.py}] Records race outcomes, maintains win rankings, logs race history, and credits prize money to the inventory cash balance.
  \item[\textbf{mission\_planning.py}] Assigns missions and verifies all required crew roles are available before the mission can start.
  \item[\textbf{garage.py}] \textit{(Bonus)} Repairs cars that exist in the inventory and maintains a repair log.
  \item[\textbf{sponsorship.py}] \textit{(Bonus)} Manages sponsors and tracks total sponsorship funds separately from race prize money.
\end{description}

Each module was verified individually before integration testing began.

% ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section*{\textcolor{blue}{Module Interaction Rules (Business Logic)}}
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
  \item A crew member must be registered before a role can be assigned.
  \item Only members with the \texttt{driver} role may enter a race.
  \item Cars must exist in inventory before a race or repair can use them.
  \item Race results must update the cash balance in Inventory.
  \item Missions cannot start if any required role is unavailable.
  \item A damaged-car repair mission must verify a mechanic is available before proceeding.
\end{itemize}

\section*{\textcolor{blue}{Errors Found and Fixed}}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Error 1 (Integration):} \textit{RaceManagement lets non-drivers race.}
    \texttt{race\_management.py} was missing the \texttt{if member.role != "driver"} check, allowing mechanics and other roles to enter races. Detected by Test 16.
    Fixed in commit: \texttt{"Error 1 (Integration): Enforce driver role check in race\_management"}.

  \item \textbf{Error 2 (Integration):} \textit{Prize money not credited to Inventory.}
    \texttt{results.py} logged the race in history and updated rankings but never called \texttt{self.inventory.update\_cash(prize)}, causing all prize money to disappear. Detected by Test 19.
    Fixed in commit: \texttt{"Error 2 (Integration): Fixed Results module to correctly distribute prize money back to Inventory cash balance"}.
\end{enumerate}
