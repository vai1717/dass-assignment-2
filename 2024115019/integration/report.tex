\section*{Implemented Modules}
\begin{itemize}
	\item \textbf{registration.py}: Handles registration of crew members and their roles.
	\item \textbf{crew\_management.py}: Assigns roles and manages crew skills.
	\item \textbf{inventory.py}: Manages cars, parts, tools, and cash.
	\item \textbf{race\_management.py}: Ensures only registered drivers can enter races with valid cars.
	\item \textbf{results.py}: Records race results and updates cash. (Bonus: maintains rankings and history)
	\item \textbf{mission\_planning.py}: Assigns missions only if required roles are available. (Bonus: supports multi-role missions)
	\item \textbf{sponsorship.py}: Manages sponsors and funds. (Bonus: tracks total funds)
	\item \textbf{garage.py}: Repairs cars present in inventory.
	\item \textbf{main.py}: Entry point, coordinates all modules and enforces business rules.
\end{itemize}

\section*{Module Descriptions}
\begin{description}
	\item[registration.py] Registers crew members and their roles.
	\item[crew\_management.py] Assigns and manages crew roles and skills.
	\item[inventory.py] Manages all cars, parts, tools, and cash for the team.
	\item[race\_management.py] Handles race creation, ensures only drivers with valid cars can participate.
	\item[results.py] Records race outcomes, updates cash, maintains rankings and history.
	\item[mission\_planning.py] Assigns missions, checks for required roles, supports multi-role missions.
	\item[sponsorship.py] Manages sponsors, tracks and sums sponsorship funds.
	\item[garage.py] Repairs cars, ensures only inventory cars are repaired.
	\item[main.py] Integrates all modules, enforces business rules, and coordinates system flow.
\end{description}
